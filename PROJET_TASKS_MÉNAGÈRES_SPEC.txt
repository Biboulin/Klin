MITIGATION:

IMPACT: High

- Archive groups (not delete)

IMPACT: Medium
MITIGATION:
- Build community first (network effect)
- Add integrations early (Slack, Calendar)
- Keep shipping fast

RISK 4: Monetization weak (8% conversion not enough)
IMPACT: Medium
MITIGATION:
- Test "Premium" positioning
- Offer "Team plan" (cheaper for groups)
- B2B2C: Student housing partnerships

IMPACT: Low (early stage)
MITIGATION:
- PostgreSQL (not MongoDB) for relational data
- Caching (Redis) for dashboard
- CDN for photos (Cloudinary)
